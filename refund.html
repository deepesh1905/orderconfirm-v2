Refund and Cancellation Policy

Last updated: May 2026

1. Cancellation Policy

You can cancel your account at any time. However, cancellations will only stop future wallet deductions. We do not cancel orders that have already been dispatched through our API.

2. Refund Policy

For SaaS wallet recharges: If you are not satisfied with our service, you can request a refund of your unused wallet balance within 7 days of the recharge date.

Used wallet balance (deducted for sent WhatsApp messages or calls) is strictly non-refundable as the API costs have already been incurred.

3. Process for Refund

To request a refund, please contact us at [Aapka Email ID]. Refunds will be processed and credited back to the original payment method within 5-7 working days.
